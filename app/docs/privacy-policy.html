Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 January 2026

Who we are

This app (“St Albans City Academy – GotSport Tracker”) is a personal hobby project created for convenience. It is not an official app of St Albans City FC, St Albans City Academy, or GotSport.

What data the app uses

  • Public match data: The app downloads publicly available fixtures, results, and standings from the GotSport website (https://system.gotsport.com/). It does not access any private or logged-in GotSport data.
  • Your team selection: The app stores your chosen team (from the club’s list on GotSport) locally on your device so it can show the correct table and fixtures when you open the app again.
  • Cached data: Tables, results, fixtures, team list, and club crest images fetched from GotSport are stored in a local cache on your device to improve load times. This cached data stays on your device and is not sent to any server.

Data we do not collect

  • No account creation or login
  • No collection of names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact lists
  • No tracking across apps or websites
  • No analytics SDKs (such as Google Analytics or Facebook SDK)

Third parties

The app displays data retrieved from GotSport. Your use of GotSport is also subject to GotSport’s own terms of use and privacy policy.

Permissions

The app does not request access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, or photos. It only needs network access to download public fixture and table information from GotSport.

Children

The app is intended to help parents/guardians and club staff follow youth team fixtures and results. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
