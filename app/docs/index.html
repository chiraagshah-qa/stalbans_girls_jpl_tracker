St Albans City Academy – GotSport Tracker

Track league tables, fixtures, and results for St Albans City FC Academy girls’ teams (U14, U16 and more) from GotSport.

About the app

This app is a personal hobby project, not an official app of St Albans City FC, St Albans City Academy, or GotSport. It pulls publicly available match data from GotSport so parents, guardians, and supporters can quickly see tables, results, and fixtures on their phone.

Features

  • Home – League position, last result, next fixture, and today’s or upcoming fixtures
  • Table – Full league table (played, won, drawn, lost, goals for/against, goal difference, points) with team crests
  • Results – Recent results with opponent crests
  • Fixtures – Fixtures for the next 15 days with home/away crests and scores
  • Settings – Choose your age group (e.g. U14 Girls, U16 Girls); your selection is stored locally on your device

Data and privacy

The app uses only public GotSport data and stores your team choice on your device. It does not collect personal information, use analytics, or require an account. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
